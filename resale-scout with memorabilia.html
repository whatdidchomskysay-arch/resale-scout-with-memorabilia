What's it worth?

Upload photos of books, coins, or sports memorabilia. AI identifies each item and estimates resale value — market price, quick-turn price, and time to sell.

Grid tip — Place coins on a numbered surface and take two photos: obverse (heads) then reverse (tails). Upload both. The AI matches by grid number, assigns a Sheldon scale grade, and values each coin individually.
Memorabilia tip — Works best with one item per photo, or a small flat lay of related items. For programs and publications, photograph the cover clearly and include a second shot of the spine and back if condition matters. Include any signatures in frame. The AI assesses event significance, featured players, era, condition, and signatures.
